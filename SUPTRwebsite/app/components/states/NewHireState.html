Employee Expectations
  1. Patient care is our number one priority.
  2. Our Goal is to provide quality patient care. This means floating patients through their plan of care safely and efficiently. Your main responsability is to facilitate smooth transition for each patient from one exercise to another.
  3. You are expected to learn simple Medical Terms used in the office and learn exercises so you can show and correct patients correct form. This includes communicating with Physical Therapist about patients status.
  4. You will be expected to learn to fit braces. You will be required to familiarize yourself with each brace and how to fit it correctly.
